## LONG DESCRIPTION (500 words — for CoinGecko, CMC, etc.)
CrossLedger is a blockchain-powered trade infrastructure platform designed to modernise cross-border commodity trade. Built on Ethereum, CrossLedger eliminates the friction, cost, and counterparty risk inherent in traditional trade finance by replacing paper-based letters of credit and manual bank reconciliation with on-chain smart escrow contracts, oracle-verified documentation, and token-enabled settlement.

The CLXT utility token powers every transaction on the platform: it is required for fee settlement, staking collateral, and dispute arbitration. Token demand is directly tied to real trade volumes — not speculative market sentiment — making CLXT fundamentally different from most utility tokens in the market.

**Key Features:**
- Smart escrow: funds locked on-chain, released automatically on delivery confirmation
- Verified digital documentation: shipping data, port arrivals, and quality certificates confirmed via oracles
- Real-time trade visibility: all parties read the same on-chain record simultaneously
- Token-enabled settlement: CLXT used for fees, staking, and governance

**Target Markets:**
- Australia–Asia bulk commodities (iron ore, coal)
- East Africa agricultural exports (coffee, tea, grains)
- South America energy logistics

**Presale Details:**
- Stage 1 price: US$0.10 per CLXT
- Payment accepted: USDT on Ethereum
- Smart contract: on-chain, verified
- Vesting: ecosystem/liquidity reserves locked 24 months; team tokens locked 18 months post-launch

**Team:**
- Gui Di Nardo — Co-Founder & CEO, trade finance specialist
- Tom Young — Co-Founder & CTO, blockchain infrastructure architect

CrossLedger targets the $2.5 trillion global trade finance gap identified by the Asian Development Bank, with a focus on mid-market exporters in developing economies who are currently excluded from prime bank financing.
